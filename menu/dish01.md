1. Beef Burger
    Protein: Ground beef (chuck is best).
    The Bun: Brioche or sesame seed bun.
    Toppings: Sharp cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato slices, red onion, and pickles.
    Condiments: Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
    Seasoning: Salt and heavy black pepper.

2. Chicken Shawarma
    Protein: Chicken thighs (boneless/skinless).
    Marinade: Olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, and red pepper flakes.
    Base: Warm pita bread or flatbread.
    The "Crunch": Sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and pickled turnips or onions.
    Sauce: Tahini sauce or a thick garlic sauce (Toum).

3. Pasta Carbonara
    Pasta: Spaghetti or Rigatoni.
    Protein: Guanciale (cured pork cheek) or Pancetta.
    Dairy: Fresh eggs (mostly yolks) and Pecorino Romano cheese (or Parmesan).
    Seasoning: Plenty of freshly cracked black pepper.
    The "Liquid Gold": Starchy pasta boiling water.

4. BLT Sandwich

    The Trinity: Bacon (thick-cut), Lettuce (iceberg or romaine for crunch), and Tomato (ripe beefsteak).
    Bread: Toasted sourdough or white Pullman loaf.
    Spread: A generous layer of mayonnaise.
    Seasoning: A pinch of salt and pepper on the tomatoes.

5. Corndog Hot Dog
    The Core: All-beef hot dogs or frankfurters.
    The Batter: Cornmeal, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    Wet Ingredients: Milk, eggs, and a little honey (optional).
    For Frying: Neutral oil (canola or vegetable) and wooden skewers.
