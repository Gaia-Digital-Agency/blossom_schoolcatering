# Welcome to Blossom School Catering 🌸

Blossom makes school meal ordering simple — register once, order anytime, from the web or WhatsApp.

---

## Getting Started

### Register on the Website
Visit **https://blossomcatering.online/** and click **Register**.
Fill in your family details and add your child (up to 5 students per family).
You will receive a login username on the registration success page — keep it safe.

### Register via WhatsApp
Send us a message saying **"register"** and we will guide you through the process.
You will need: family name, parent email, a password, and your child's school and grade.
Registration takes under 2 minutes.

---

## Website Features 🖥️

- **Browse the menu** — view daily meals by session (Breakfast, Lunch, Snack)
- **Place & manage orders** — order for one or multiple children, edit before cutoff
- **Billing & payments** — upload proof of payment, download receipts
- **Order history** — full history with quick reorder
- **Family hub** — manage linked students and account details

👉 **https://blossomcatering.online/**

---

## WhatsApp Features 💬

Chat with **Brian**, your Blossom assistant, anytime:

- 🍽️ **Place an order** — just say what you want and for which child
- ❌ **Cancel an order** — ask Brian to delete an order before cutoff
- 📋 **Check today's order** — ask "what's my order today"
- 📅 **Bulk orders** — order for the whole week or month in one message
- 🔔 **Daily reminders** — receive a morning notification of today's meals
- 📝 **Register** — say "register" to create a new family account
