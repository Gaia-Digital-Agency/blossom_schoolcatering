# Terms & Condition User Guide

## Ordering Rules
- Orders follow service date/session controls
- Weekend and blackout date restrictions are enforced
- Cutoff time rules are enforced for placement and edits

## Account and Access
- Role-based login routes are enforced
- Unauthorized access redirects to matching login pages

## Data and Operational Notes
- Billing, kitchen, and delivery statuses are tracked per order flow
- Audit-sensitive actions should be performed by authorized roles only
